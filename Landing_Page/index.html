Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Click one of the buttons at the top of the page: Program Catalog or Volunteering Event Catalog. These two are databases for students in NYC.

Can you make an article about X?

Our blog writers are always researching more about the process and compiling information. We will try to expand this resource page as much as possible. But being a small group, we need support for that. Email us with what you would want us to write about next.

Will there be program catalogs for other cities?

As we expand, we will had more and more cities, but this is a wikipedia type of thing where we need your input to make something largely scalable. If we receive requests to upload programs from several different cities, then we can together, as a community, build a list of enough information to help everyone. So yes, we will in due time.

What if I can't find a program in my location?

There are a limited amount of programs avaialble. Be flexible by allowing more locations for more results. Most programs are in Manhattan. We constantly check for new programs, and label them as new.

How do I apply the filters in the program catalog?

Above the catalog, you will find basic instructions to navigate and search according to your needs. If you still have questions, email us. In short, you need to use the search bar.

How do I get notifications about programs or new blog articles?

Email us at preplify@gmail.com with the subect "NOTIFICATIONS". That will automatically put you on our email list for notifying about new programs we added.

How do I host programs?

On the program catalog page, you will find a plus sign button in the top right corner which will take you to a google form to fill out information about the program.

What if my question isn't in the faq?

Please contact us via email. Our team will get to you as soon as possible. We should be able to respond within a couple of days at latest. Contact info provided below, but email gets quickest reply.
