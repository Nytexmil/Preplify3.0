Services

With InfiSearch, users can freely navigate through our website to find internships, volunteering opportunities, mentorships, and other programs, all in New York City. For a lot of teens, transitioning from education to the real world is a rocky one. InfiSearch is here to help others realize the sheer number of opportunities around them to take that next step in life. If the user is having a hard time searching for a program, they can always use our filter tool to filter based on interest, benefits, company, location, and timing!


You can add several filters to make your search more applicable to you and your needs!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

At the top of this page, you will find a button for Program Catalog and Volunteering Events. Both are databases of events and programs. The volunteering button is specific to members of those organizations. The Program catalog is specific to users who want to learn about the most productive and worthwhile porgrams in NYC, including mentorships, internships, research, and camps.

What if I can't find anything in my borough or time slot?

There are a limited amount of programs avaialble; try to be more flexible by allowing more locations and times to get more results. Most of the programs are in Manhattan. We also constantly check for new programs, so sign up for notifications to receive an email when we find a new program that fits your needs.

How do I apply the filters in the program catalog?

Above the catalog, you will find basic instructions to navigate and search according to your needs. If you still have questions, utilize the "Contact Us" Form.

How do I get program notifications/updates?

Use the Contact Form at the bottom of this page. For the subject, put "NOTIFICATIONS". That will automatically put you on our email list for notifying about new programs we added.

How do I host programs?

On the program catalog page, you will find a plus sign button in the top right corner which will take you to a google form to fill out information about the program you want to add to the catalog. Our team manually verifies each program before hosting to the catalog. You can find more specific details in the actual google form.

What if my question isn't in the faq?

Please contact us via email in the box below. Our team will get to you as soon as possible. We should be able to respond within a couple of days at latest.
